About Producer Activity Boot Camps
​Attending one of our boot camps is the best way to on-board a new producer or re- energize a vested producer.  Producer Activity's boot camps are not fluffy seminars, they are roll-up-your-sleeves camps that give producers the tools and skills they need to create activity immediately.
What Does A Camp Include?
Our 6-month program and 5-day boot camp include coaching as outlined below:
  • Understand the mind of the prospect and the mind of the producer
  • Gain a complete understanding of the science and art of prospecting
  • Receive an arsenal of great content for P&C and Benefits campaigns
  • Learn the formula to write successful content on your own
  • Make (many) live calls and emails with me during time onsite
  • Work through any issues and reluctance
  • Learn where to find prospects and researching tools
  • Receive ongoing monthly support throughout the 6-month period
